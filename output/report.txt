Pandemic Impact Analysis Report
========================================

Most affected country (total cases): United States
Highest cases per million: Brunei
Highest death rate: Yemen

Key Insights:
- Countries with large populations show high total cases.
- Smaller countries dominate per capita metrics.
- Death rates vary significantly across countries.
- Per capita analysis provides better comparison than total values.

Conclusion:
Population size plays a crucial role in pandemic spread, but per capita metrics reveal the true severity.
